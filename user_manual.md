Login
*****

1) Go to localhost:5000 and you should be automatically directed to the login page.

2) If you have an account, type your username and password into the field and press 'Login'

Register
********

1) On the login page, press the link that says register. This will direct you to the register page.

2) Enter a unique username

3) Enter your email and password

4) Press 'Register' button

5) Now you will be directed to login page to login with your new account

Submit a ticket
***************

1) On your dashboard, press the 'New Ticket' link in the navbar

2) Enter the title, descripition and prioirty level of the ticket you are submitting

3) Choose what status your ticket is at

4) Press 'Submit Ticket' button

5) You should get a confirmation message and be able to see this ticket now in your dashboard

Dashboard
*********

For a regular user you will see your tickets that you have created. 
You will be able to edit your tickets.

For an admin, you will see all user's tickets. You can edit and delete any ticket.

Edit Ticket
***********

1) Press the 'edit' button on the dashboard beside the ticket you want to edit.

2) The values on the form are pre-populated, so change the value you wish to change on the ticket.

3) Press 'Update Ticket' button

Delete Ticket (Admin Only)
**************************

1) Press the 'delete' button on the dashboard beside the ticket you want to delete.

2) Press 'OK' on the pop up to ensure your happy deleting this ticket.

3) You should get a confirmation message and the ticket will have disappeared from the dashboard.

Promote/Demote User (Admin Only)
********************************

1) Press the 'Promote User' tab on the navbar

2) Press 'Promote' or 'Demote' next to the user you want to promote/demote

3) Press 'OK on the pop up to ensure your happy promoting/demoting this user.

4) You should get a confirmation message and the user will have a different account type in the table.
